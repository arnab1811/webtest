Our Mission

South Sudan Seed Hub is dedicated to transforming the seed sector in South Sudan. Our mission is to provide comprehensive information and foster collaboration among seed stakeholders. We aim to create a platform that facilitates dialogue, shares knowledge, and promotes innovation in the seed sector.

Our Objectives

Objective 1: "To provide a comprehensive analysis of the seed sector in South Sudan, highlighting the challenges and opportunities.

Objective 2: "To outline strategic pathways towards a robust seed sector, detailing the ambitions, strategic actions, and key stakeholders for each pathway.

Objective 3: To create a Seed Portal that provides access to seed-related information, policies, regulatory provisions, and requirements.

Objective 4: "To foster collaboration among seed stakeholders through events and interactive platforms."
